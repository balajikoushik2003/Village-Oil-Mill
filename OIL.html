About Us

Village Oil Mill is dedicated to offering traditionally processed, chemical-free food products. Our cold-pressed oils, stone-ground flours, and ethically sourced millets and spices promote well-being and sustainability. Rooted in tradition and committed to purity, we are here to help you embrace natural living.
