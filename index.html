About Us

At Village Oil Mill, we believe in the power of nature. Our products are crafted with care, using traditional methods that preserve the natural goodness of ingredients. We are committed to providing you with the highest quality oils, flours, and snacks that are free from chemicals and additives.

Our mission is to promote healthy living through natural foods. We source our ingredients from local farmers who share our values of sustainability and ethical practices. By choosing Village Oil Mill, you are not just nourishing your body; you are also supporting local communities and preserving traditional farming methods.

Village Oil Mill is dedicated to offering traditionally processed, chemical-free food products. Our cold-pressed oils, stone-ground flours, and ethically sourced millets and spices promote well‑being and sustainability. Rooted in tradition and committed to purity, we are here to help you embrace natural living.
