About Us

Village Oil Mill is dedicated to offering traditionally processed, chemical-free food products. Our cold-pressed oils, stone-ground flours, and ethically sourced millets and spices promote well-being and sustainability. Rooted in tradition and committed to purity, we are here to help you embrace natural living.

🔍 Vision

• To be a trusted provider of pure, traditionally processed food products, fostering a healthier lifestyle through authenticity, sustainability, and ethical practices.
• To revive and sustain indigenous food wisdom by integrating traditional practices with modern awareness for long-term wellness.

🎯 Mission

• To offer nutrient-rich, chemical-free oils, flours, spices, and millets.
• To inspire wholesome living through traditional food choices.
• To support local farmers by sourcing responsibly.
• To uphold transparency and quality in every product.
• To revive and promote ancient food processing techniques.
